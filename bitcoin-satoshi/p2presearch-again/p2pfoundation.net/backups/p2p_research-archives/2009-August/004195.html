> It never ceases to annoy me that people like Parenti take the "free
> market" rhetoric of corporate capitalist apologetics at face value:
>
> >   In sum, free-market corporate capitalism is by its nature a disaster

> "free market corporate capitalism" is by its nature a contradiction in
> terms.  It is corporate capitalist precisely to the extent that it is
> not a free market.
>

> as denouncing Stalinist Russia as "socialism"--something Parenti no
> doubt would object to loudly and strenuously from know-nothings on the
> right.

> mass production:
>
> Its essence is the transformation of living nature into
> > mountains of commodities and commodities into heaps of dead capital.
