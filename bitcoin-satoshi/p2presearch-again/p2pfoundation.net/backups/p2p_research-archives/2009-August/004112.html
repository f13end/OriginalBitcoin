For a call for "socializing markets," see Leo Panitch, *Renewing socialism:
Democracy, strategy, and imagination*, Boulder, CO: Westview, 2001, pp.
223-224. For the complete argument, see Diane Elson, "Socializing markets,
not market socialism," especially pp. 80-82, in Leo Panitch and Colin Lay,
Eds., *The socialist register 1999*, London: Merlin, 1998.
