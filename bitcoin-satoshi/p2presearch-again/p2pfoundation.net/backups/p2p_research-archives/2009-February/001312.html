The FSF is organizing an open conference to discuss Free Network Services
and other high-priority projects. The event will happen from 21-22 Mar 2009
in Cambridge, at the Harvard Science Center. Details in FSF blog post:

http://www.fsf.org/blogs/membership/libreplanet2009pre

And on the groups.fsf.org wiki:

http://groups.fsf.org/index.php/LibrePlanet2009

This event is, as far as I know, the first of its kind, and I'm really
looking forward to getting all people interested in the topic working
together to make this a productive event.
