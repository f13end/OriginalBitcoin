This is perhaps the most interesting approach to understand the crisis of capitalsim I have come across.  Rick Wolff says it's not a financial crisis, the core problem is not money, interest, speculation etc.. It is also not about neoliberalism and deregulation of markets, the core of the problem is the classical Marxist take of the tensions between owners of means of production and workers.
All very well explained. I am not an economist, but the analysis is convincing.
