A giant corporation or any corporation is a walled entity itself, so I
see a contradiction at the philosophical level whenever some
corporation talks abou tearing down walls.

Like Reagan tearing down the Berlin wall only to see the whole world
fall inside the walls of capitalism.

But if you leave it up to Google there is always a catch somewhere.
Some way for them to have more power or more money while pushing for
progressive agenda on the surface.

I do appreciate PageRank, but their patent of FriendRank is extrememly
troubling.
