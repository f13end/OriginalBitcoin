Subject: Abundance discussion group formed at Google Groups




I invited this list to Abundance at Google Groups:
http://groups.google.com/group/postscarcity

The invitation list is pending approval, so I thought I'd take the liberty
in informing you sooner. I apologize to anyone not interested in the topic.
A discussion group will attenuate that issue. The discussion list will help
bridge various viewpoints toward the same goal. I believe a variety of
methods and perspectives will be needed to generate material independence,
prosperity, freedom, abundance, and the other words that describe
post-scarcity.

Please forward this to other individuals and groups you think may be
interested.
