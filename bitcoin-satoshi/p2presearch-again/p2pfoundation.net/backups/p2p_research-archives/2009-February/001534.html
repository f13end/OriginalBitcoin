I want to add that when change happens, it takes everyone, even the
most prepared by surprise.

That is why it's called "CHANGE"

Any action that is _planned_ and _advertised_ **before** it happens is
just *theatrics*

contributing to it. We cannot hope that we control it.

I need to read what the great philosophers had to say about this
because the word "change" is thrown around lightly these days, and for
profit and greed.
