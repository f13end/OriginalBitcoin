BBC World Debate "Food - who pays the price"

The discussion highlights the antagonisms between small holders and
industrial agriculture in context of growing issues of global fuel and food
supply.

http://permaculture.org.au/2008/06/28/bbc-world-debate-food-who-pays-the-price/

I also saw this program on television not so long ago

Super Rich: The Greed Game

Its about the global financial system and the credit crunch who benefited
and how. With some explanation of what Private Equity and Hedge Funds are.
For someone who never got an education on how these things work the program
is quite illuminating.
