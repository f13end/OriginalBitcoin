Hello,

The Transition Network is an organisation whos purpose is -
"To dramatically reduce carbon emissions and massively increase resilience
for all aspects of life that our communities, businesses and organisations
need in order to sustain themselves and thrive"

They have just released -
The Transition Network: a Proposal for Structure.
http://transitionculture.org/2008/07/22/shaping-the-transition-network-your-thoughts-please/

They have secured some funding and are looking for a Knowledge Management
Person for the development of the online community side of things.

They are interested in ideas, consultation and feedback on their proposals.

Reading through the first few pages of the document their are many areas of
interest and crossover with the P2P Foundation. Particularly in terms of
social change and in different models of social organisation.
