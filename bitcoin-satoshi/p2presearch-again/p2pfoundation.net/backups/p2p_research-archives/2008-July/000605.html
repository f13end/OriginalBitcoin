Third Access to Knowledge Global Conference** ** Yale ISP will convene its
third annual global conference on Access to Knowledge (A2K3) on September
8-10, 2008.

This year's conference will be held at the Geneva International Conference
Centre <http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7154.htm> and is organized
in collaboration with an international team of
partners<http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7156.htm>.
The event is free and open to the public.

   - Overview <http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7150.htm>
   - Registration <http://www.regonline.com/63341_603819M>
   - Schedule <http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7160.htm>
   - Panels
    <http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7158.htm>
   - Call for Papers <http://www.law.yale.edu/intellectuallife/7044.htm>
