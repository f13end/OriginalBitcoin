The wiki is now increasingly graced with documents that claim:

"The content of this page is an official policy approved by the P2P
Foundation Board of Trustees. This policy may not be circumvented, eroded,
or ignored on local P2P Foundation"
like http://p2pfoundation.net/Pledge_of_personal_commitment

Please take not that these are no such things. They may be a good idea or
not, but at this stage are simply proposals by Mr. Alex Rollin.

I have lost the capability to check the contributions by Alex and the
numerous protocol and policy pages, worthy of a large multinational. They
could be useful one day.

My problem is that there are not labelled as such, but would appear to
outsiders as board approved material.

So, for your info, they are not, in fact, there are NONE such pages on our
wiki.


Alex, thanks for labelling such pages as what they are, proposals,
