Dear advisors,

Good news!

We're in the running to host a panel about the Sharing Industry at the
influential conference about innovation, SXSW Interactive.  People from
around the globe come to Austin each March to learn about the latest trends.
 I can't overstate it's importance.

The foundation of our panel is an first-time-ever survey on the Sharing
Economy conducted with Latitude Research.  This will be augmented with
commentary by leading entrepreneurs in the space including John Zimmer of
Zimride.com and Micki Krimmel of Neighborgoods.net.  The survey was designed
to help social innovators develop successful sharing enterprises.

So, we have something important to share at SXSW.  Please help our panel get
accepted by voting for it today at this link:

 http://panelpicker.sxsw.com/ideas/view/5774

And please ask you friends to vote too.  Here's a message you could use on
your blogs, social media, e-mail lists, and online forums:

*Please vote for Shareable's SXSW panel on The New Sharing Economy:
http://bit.ly/bq9rV5 Panel will share info to help sharing startups grow!*

Panel acceptance is very competitive, so please vote today.  I'll keep you
posted on our progress.
