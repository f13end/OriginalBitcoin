 Subject: Bradley Manning Support Network - libertarian outreach

 Dear libertarian friends,

 For the past couple of months, I’ve been developing the Bradley
 Manning Support Network (www.bradleymanning.org) in support of accused
 WikiLeaks whistleblower Pfc. Bradley Manning.


 Reaction thus far has been strong. However, the dedicated folks I’ve
 been privileged to work with the most closely fall largely on the
 traditional “left” part of the political spectrum. I’d very much like
 to recruit support among libertarian circles as well, which is the
 reason for this letter.


 If you would like to get involved, there are a range of options:

 ·         A personal endorsement, to be published at
 www.bradleymanning.org/about/supporters

 ·         Organizational endorsement from orgs you work with

 ·         Membership on our Advisory Board

 ·         Mention, linkage and interviews for your publication(s)

 ·         Event organization and/or support for our International Days
 of Action, Sept 16-18 (http://www.bradleymanning.org/days-of-action/),
 as well as elsewhere

 ·         Press release forwarding

 ·         Referral of this message to other similarly-minded
 individuals and organizations

 ·         And so on


 Whatever your level of interest, I would welcome the chance to work
 with you for this cause. I’m happy to provide more information. Please
 get in touch.



 Peace,
