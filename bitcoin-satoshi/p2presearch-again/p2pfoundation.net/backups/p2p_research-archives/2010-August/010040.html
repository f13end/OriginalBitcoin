> The Labor Theory of Property can be summarized easily: Let the party
> that appropriates property be the party responsible for its creation.
> Only people (labor) can bear responsibility unlike thinks such as
> machinery, money, land, raw material, etc. Hence the workers should
> appropriate anything produced as a result of their labor. It follows
> that the workers should then acquire any profit or loss resulting from
> the sale of the goods they appropriated. That is the basis for worker
> ownership of all businesses.
>
> If the workers are financially responsible for their business they
> should determine in how it operates. In addition, basic democratic
> theory allows positive governing control (the ability to compel
> action) only with the consent of the governed. Both arguments support
> democratic workplaces.
