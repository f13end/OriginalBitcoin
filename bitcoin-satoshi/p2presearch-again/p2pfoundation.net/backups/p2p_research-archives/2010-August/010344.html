I work with the p2p Foundation, and head the Gruppo Salìngaros in

of Texas at San Antonio, and am on the faculty of other universities

      4) specific info on your expertise concerning specific

I am one of the pioneers helping to define a new discipline:
PEER-TO-PEER URBANISM. As a leading urbanist, I bring experience in
scientific methods of urbanism, participatory design, network
connectivity applied to urban structure, and biophilia.
