I agree that the actual text of the page gives no indication that it's
a mere proposal by one individual, who the author is, or that the page
is not an official policy document of the P2P Foundation.

I am aware that it's possible to ascertain the authorship of an edit
or a page by going to History.  But a casual reader should be able to
determine the authorship without having to do this.  I believe anyone
adding text to the Wiki should make it clear, with a byline in the
text itself, where it came from.
