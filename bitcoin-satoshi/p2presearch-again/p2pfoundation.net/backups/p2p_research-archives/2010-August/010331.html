It's been a long time coming, this Ning thing, and lots was written and
discussed on the subject.

Inspired by Josef's wishes to enable the social site to continue on, and
pressed for time:

http://peerforge.com

and the attached free software social networking site:

http://commons.peerforge.com (running the new "Drupal Commons" distribution
from Acquia)

something different (like tomorrow, if you like :)

gladly recreate some of the 100 posts from Ning in the next couple days.

I feel like, if there are people who feel strongly like I do, that there

maybe folks will want to give it a try.
