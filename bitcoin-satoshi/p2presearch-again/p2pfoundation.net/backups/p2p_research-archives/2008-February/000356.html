Here you have a short description of my project to post. If it is to long I
can shorten it a bit.


How can small businesses use open knowledge to leverage their business ?

I am starting a project which is to help small businesses explore the
possibilities within open knowledge and mass collaboration.
I believe that this is an area where you can do something good while using
business sense and making more money. I have experienced talking to small
businesses how think the ideas sound interesting, but they don't know how to
learn more or carry it out in practise.

The objective is create a tool kit that can help smaller companies figure
out what this is all about, an how they should carry it out.

If you know of businesses of projects that could be relevant for this
project, i would be grateful for sugestions. peter at businessofopenness.org
To find out more see www.businessofopenness.org
