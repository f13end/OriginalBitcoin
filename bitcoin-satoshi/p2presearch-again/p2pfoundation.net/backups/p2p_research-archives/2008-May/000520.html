US National Science Foundation (NSF) joins EUROHESC

US researchers are now able to apply for collaborative projects with their
European colleagues

US scientists interested in working in an international collaborative
research project on "Higher Education and Social Change" are now asked to
contact their European colleagues to prepare an application. Likewise
European researchers who were intending to include US collaborators can now
do so.

*The deadline for submission of outline proposal is on 15 May 2008* (12.00
hrs CET).

EUROCORES funding comes from national funding organisations, hence national
requirements may apply and this may result in additional material to be
provided along with the joint application to ESF. US researchers are
therefore kindly invited to contact Dr. Diane Spresser, Office of the
Assistant Director, Directorate for Education and Human Resources, NSF,
703-292-5118, dspresse at nsf.gov at EHR to find out more.

All details regarding the joint application to ESF (including call,
submission form, guidelines, FAQ and glossary) can be found at
www.esf.org/eurohesc <http://www.icahdq.org/news/www.esf.org/eurohesc>.
Please study this material carefully!

In case of further questions, please contact Dr. Frank Kuhn (fkuhn at esf.org)
at ESF.
