For the next three years, the Focuss.Info Initiative offers two scenarios
for the execution of the Initiative and promotion of Web 2.0 technologies
such as social bookmarking:

1. Funding for an Information Professional from the Global South for five
days per week, and;
2. Funding for an Information Professional from the Global North for five
days per week

The Information Professional will induce and promote new Web technologies in
favor of community building in global development cooperation. The
Focuss.Info coordinator is favoring the scenario with an information
professional from the Global South. Within this scenario, the Initiative is
able to pro-actively encourage and mobilize partner organizations and
individual practitioners from the more local areas in the Global South.
Additionally, it gives the Global South the possibility of professionally
working with the issue: knowledge creation and sharing
 If you have more questions about the two scenarios or if you are interested
in funding the Focuss.Info Initiative, please contact the coordinator of the
Focuss.Info Initiative by sending an email to *ask at focuss.info*
