deeply, or even being replaced with something different? What would our
society be like then?

With our maverick<http://www.yi-tan.com/wiki/yi-tan/learning_from_mavericks?wikiPageId=634355>guest
Polly

   - How might a post-capitalist world be different from today's

   ?
   - Where will the change be felt the sharpest? Scarcity vs. abundance?
   - What signposts will tell us that this is major change, not a small
   evolution?
   - Are there places<http://www.amazon.com/Nowtopia-Programmers-Bicyclists-Vacant-Lot-Gardeners/dp/1904859771>where
post-consumer values already rule?
