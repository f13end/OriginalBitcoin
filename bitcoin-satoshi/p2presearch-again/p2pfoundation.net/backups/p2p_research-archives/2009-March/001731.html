I've commented before on the proliferation of web-based barter and
currency systems, and their failure to coalesce around a common
architecture or set of standards.

Here's a web-based system designed by Saren Calvert.
http://www.lets-software.net/   The features are described here:
http://www.lets-software.net/features/

This strikes me as a beautifully designed system.  It's transparent
with information (including advertisements of goods and services,
member trading histories and ratings, etc.) publicly accessible.

It's been adopted by VicLETS, the Victoria BC LETS system.
