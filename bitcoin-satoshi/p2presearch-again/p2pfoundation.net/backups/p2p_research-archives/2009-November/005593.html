Of course all morality is subjective.  I suppose murder can be justified by
an individual.  In the end, we decide how much normative factors move us.
"Human rights" are often laughed at by despots who say they are irrelevant
to them.

But it seems odd to hate corporations, hate the rich, hate power, and then
to take resources from the same quite willingly.  It seems to me that
government support seriously compromises the wing of P2P that tends to
despise wealth, profit and power.

I work for government and am a great believer.  So I find it ethical for
myself.  I find it deeply unethical for those who taunt the market to take
government resources in a market system.

Ryan
