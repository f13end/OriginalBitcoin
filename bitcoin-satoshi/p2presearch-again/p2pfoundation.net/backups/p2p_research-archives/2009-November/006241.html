Dear friends,

I have been asked to feed my twitter to identica, but not sure how ...

this is what I found, see below, from http://status.net/wiki/Apps#Posting

any recommended tool?

See


   - HelloTxt <http://hellotxt.com/> - Post updates to Identi.ca and other
   services.
   - Ping.fm <http://ping.fm/> - Post updates to Identi.ca and other
   services. PingFire
<https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/8365>is a Firefox
extension for Ping.fm.
   - Pixelpipe <http://pixelpipe.com/> Post messages and media to over 50
   different services simultaneously trough the website, cell phone/mobile,
   e-mail, or one of a number of free apps (for media).
   - switchAbit <http://switchabit.com/> - Sends messages from one
   service/feed to one or many others. Can be automatic or on a post-by-post
   basis. Supports identi.ca, Twitter, Blogger, Facebook, Flickr, Jaiku,
   Omeego, Tumblr, WordPress, and Feeds. If you want to automatically crosspost
   on Twitter and a Laconica install (like identi.ca) you should use
   StatusNet's built in function for this, because switchAbit is quite slow.
   - updating.me - Post updates to Identi.ca and other services. Beta code:
   identicapublictimeln
   - PostLater <http://www.postlater.com/> - Schedule future-dated status
   updates for any StatusNet based site and many others. Also supports
   recurring entries. Costs $19.95 a month!
   - twitterfeed <http://twitterfeed.com/> - Automatically update your
   Identi.ca or Twitter account from any (RSS or Atom) feed.
   - Pingvine <http://pingvine.com/> - Automatically update your Identi.ca
   or Twitter account from any (RSS or Atom) feed.
   - WebDevStudio's identi.ca to Twitter
tool<http://webdevstudios.com/update-twitter-from-your-identica-account-automatically/>-
Automatically forwards anything you post on
   identi.ca to Twitter, with a customizable flag.
   - OpenMicroBlogging Facebook
app<http://www.facebook.com/apps/application.php?id=29521195395>-
Automatically update Facebook via OMB
   - Identi.ca's Facebook app <http://apps.facebook.com/identica/> -
   Automatically update Facebook with your latest Identi.ca notices, post to
   Identi.ca from Facebook, etc.
