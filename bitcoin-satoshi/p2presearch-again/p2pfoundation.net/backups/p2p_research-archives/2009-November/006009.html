Nishant Shah sent a message to the members of The Centre for Internet and
Society.

--------------------
Subject: Digital Natives with a Cause?

The Centre for Internet and Society, Bangalore and Hivos have assessed the
state of knowledge on the potential impact of youth for social
transformation and political engagement in the South.
This report ‘Digital Natives with a Cause?’ charts scholarship and practice
of youth and technology and informs further research and intervention within
diverse contexts and cultures.

The report is now available
at http://www.facebook.com/l/85138;cis-india.org/research/dn/dnrep
and at
http://www.facebook.com/l/85138;http//www.hivos.net/Hivos-Knowledge-Programme/Themes/Digital-Natives-with-a-Cause/News/New-Publication-on-Digital-Natives

We hope that you shall read, respond, share and distribute this report to a
larger community of interested stake holders.
