EcoRev 'No. 33, "Thinking after capitalism with André Gorz"
"I'm not saying that these radical changes will be realized. I only say that
for the first time, we want it come true. The means exist so that people who
are working on it methodically." Andre Gorz (2007).
Two years ago we received the last installment of our sponsor Andre Gorz,
just days before he and his wife will leave us.
This number EcoRev 'pays tribute to the thinker of the political ecology and
takes a particular form appears as ten years after our first issue:
Surviving capitalism. But this is not a tribute that seeks to sanctify "a
thought gorzienne", but rather - in a context of global systemic crisis - a
reflection that comes examine and extend the central idea of his last words:
The output of Capitalism has already begun.
This issue is also intended to show how intuitions are ever present and able
to contribute to the construction, complex but necessary, a political
ecology alternative to capitalism.
