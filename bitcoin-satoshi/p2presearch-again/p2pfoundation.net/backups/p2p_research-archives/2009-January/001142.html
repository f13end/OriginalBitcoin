Dylan Newcomb presents...
The 16 Ways: Integral Movement Patterns
January 2009 - Los Angeles Preview

Researched and developed over an eight-year-period by choreographer and
movement researcher Dylan Newcomb <http://dylannewcomb.com>, The 16-Ways is
a simple and comprehensive series of core physical movements uniquely
derived to support you in developing subtle-energy as psychological
awareness & super-acute interpersonal sensitivity! Built on the principles
of Yin and Yang, The 16-Ways integrates learnings from cognitive psychology,
personality typology, Spiral Dynamics, the I-Ching, and elements from Ken
Wilber's Integral Theory. It offers a clear and intuitive way for people to
understand different types of energy and learn how to work with them on the
levels of body, emotion and thought (derived from 8-years of research on
1,000+ subjects at DansLab <http://danslab.residentie.net/> in Holland) .

Dylan Newcomb will be presenting lectures, practices & demonstrations on the
science of the "16-Ways" all this week!

* Daily Schedule *

12:00pm-3:00pm Experience "The 16 Ways"
A new, beautifully simple and powerful way to experience and articulate
energy, the 16-Ways are simple in form, and profound in depth and effect.
Often, even upon doing them for the first time, many people report long
sought-after energetic shifts, rebalancings, increased Delta-wave activity,
and an overall enhanced awareness of their subtle-body. Over the course of
three-hours, various forms of using the 16-Ways will be given. If you feel
called to bring your physical body more deeply into your own personal and
spiritual development, this opportunity to practice a breakthrough approach
to synergizing mind, body and spirit should not be missed. (Suggest $25
Donation)

3:00pm-6:00pm Understand and Apply "The 16-Ways"
Dylan will lay out the basics of what he has discovered over his past
10-years of research into the relationship of physical movement, breath, and
vocal sounds to various fields such as psychology, sociology, Taoism,
and Integral
philosophy. Discover the essential principles of how negative and positive
(or yin and yang) charge flows through our mind-body forming cognitions,
emotions, and physical movement. Get a sense of how these polarities might
be playing out in your own personal energy dynamics, forming the core of
your thoughts, emotions and even social-actions. The form of this session
will flow freely between lecture, demonstration and Q&A. (Suggest $25
Donation)

6:00pm-9:00pm Exploring the Connections...
In the evenings, Dylan will be available to attendees who would like to
explore questions and ideas around his body of work & research while
offering hands-on ways to work with subtle-energy and one's own
'thought-forms' directly. (Free)
