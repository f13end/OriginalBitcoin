                          + Welcome     Hello Everyone,

The big news for January is that we launched the Shapeways
Shops<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Shapeways/fe0cc2fe8e/86b84b7dd7/88d234ae48>.
The Shapeways Shops are a way for you to make money on Shapeways. How does
it work? Well first of all you need to open a Shop. You choose a URL, upload
<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Shapeways/fe0cc2fe8e/86b84b7dd7/0251ee88d3>yourlogo
and decide how much you would like to make on each
model<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Shapeways/fe0cc2fe8e/86b84b7dd7/94ae94069f>(your
margin/markup)<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Shapeways/fe0cc2fe8e/86b84b7dd7/c4198a35f3>.
Once someone buys your model, we print it, ship it, do all the customer
service and pay you the margin. The rights to your designs will always
remain with you. With the Shapeways Shops you can now have your own factory
and sell physical products worldwide. Does this sound like something you
want to do? Open your own shop in a few minutes
here<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?Shapeways/fe0cc2fe8e/86b84b7dd7/231efcb2ac>.
