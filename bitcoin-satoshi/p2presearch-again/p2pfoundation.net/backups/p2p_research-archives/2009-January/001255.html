Dear P2P'er / OM'er,

Many insights, much learning and a ton of clarity promoting changes have
been incorporated into the P2P Energy Economy draft Release 2.00.0.

Thanks mostly to having had nothing else to employ my energy and the endless
and very fruitful debates I've had. In fact, there has not been a single
debate that has not been transformed into increased clarity, so thank you
all for debating.

I'm taking a 40 days off this to work on mobile development (only marginally
related to this project) so in the meantime I would love it if you could
forward any counter-arguments, feedback, moral guidance or any and all input
to the P2P Energy open discussion list at:
p2p-energy-economy at googlegroups.com
