> Hi Michel,

> I crafted a preliminary definition of open enterprise<http://www.p2pfoundation.net/Open_Enterprise>
> :
>
> *An open enterprise maximizes the involvement of every individual who is
> in any way affected by its actions. It incorporates the means necessary for
> increased involvement into its organizational structure, and actively seeks
> to minimize means of exclusion.*
>
> The key concept here is "involvement".  It is only slightly less empty
> than the term "open", but I think it points in the right direction.
>
> I agree that a matrix for sorting enterprises by openness, perhaps along
> different structural axes (ownership, control, transparency), would be
> useful.  I just wasn't able to wrap my head around it yet.
>
> -Matt
