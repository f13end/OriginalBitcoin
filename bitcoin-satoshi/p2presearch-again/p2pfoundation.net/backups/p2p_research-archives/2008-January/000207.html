Dear friends,

Perhaps you have already seen this link,
http://p2pfoundation.net/In_a_Nutshell

Mauro Biego has produced a more than excellent overview, with multiple
levels -- so that people can choose their preferred level of detail --, and
introduction to the broad peer to peer world. It will be an excellent way in
for people who are not so familiar with the complexity of the material we
have been collating.

One thing though: Mauro would really appreciate if some people could go over
his language, as he is not a native speaker. Note that it seems excellent to
me, but I'm not a native speaker either, so it would be nice if we could
have some volunteers, who each could do a few pages. I don't think this will
be a undue burden,

Michel
