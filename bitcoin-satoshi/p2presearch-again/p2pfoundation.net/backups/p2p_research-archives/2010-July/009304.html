MAKE subscriber Nick Santillan of Vancouver, BC, wrote in to share a
video of his Red Blue CNC, a prototype which we posted about a couple
months ago. The video highlights the ease of assembly and configuration
of the machine. It's assembled and disassembled with a single wrench,
and can be configured as a wheeled cart for easy transport. (Also see
it use its steppers to play the Tetris theme song.)
