Dear all

We are pleased to inform you that the *MERLION 2010 PROGRAM – Call for Joint
Franco-Singapore Research Projects Proposals* is open for application.

This call and the subsequent support for the selected projects aim to create
new, and/ or strengthen existing scientific cooperation between France and
Singapore researchers.  Projects submitted must be a collaborative research
effort involving a French and a Singapore-based partner.

Applicants are invited to submit their proposals online (and not by any
other means) to facilitate the subsequent downloading of the proposals for
evaluation.

Additional details are available at the website:
http://www.ambafrance-sg.org/sciencetech-platform/merlion/.

The deadline for the call for proposals is *31 August 2010, 5pm French Time*.
(Completed files must be sent before this date.  Files with missing
documents and information will not be examined).

All applicants are requested to ensure that they have complied with the
French Embassy’s instructions as there will be no administrative checks.
Applicants are requested to submit a duplicate copy of the full set of
application documents to: rso-external-grants at ntu.edu.sg  by 31 August
2010.

You may forward your queries, if any, to rso-external-grants at ntu.edu.sg.

Thank you.



Best regards
