Wow, thanks James. I've been getting tired of seeing people talk about
"crowd" this and "crowd" that. "Crowd" is really an extension of 20th
century industrial era thinking. Like "human resources" just
extrapolated to "crowds" of people. In the end, in the industrial era
paradigm, everything is "labor and resources" to be used and consumed,
including people.
