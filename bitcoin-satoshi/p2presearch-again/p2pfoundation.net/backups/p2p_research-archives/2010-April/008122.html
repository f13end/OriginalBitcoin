Hello there,

We all know that the more awareness there will be on ACTA, the more
chance we will get to stop it.

With this in mind, we designed a small "alert box", a very simple web
widget with a very short manifesto that anyone can put on his blog or
website. It links to a neutral website linking to major ACTA resources:

http://www.stopacta.info/alertbox
