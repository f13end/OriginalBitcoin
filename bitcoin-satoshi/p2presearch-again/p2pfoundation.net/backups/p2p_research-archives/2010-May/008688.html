  Dear all,


I have to say thank you to Franz, Michel and Robin Good for giving me the
chance to make some step forward about the idea of  the *"1st wikiTV
Festival G99 proposals for a sustainable World, L'Aquila, 8 - 10 July 2010"*,
link:

http://www.laquila99.tv/wiki/index.php?title=1st_wikiTV_Festival_G99_proposals_for_a_sustainable_World


The wikiTV Festival will be only ON-LINE with the wish to collect open
digital contributions and to have "p2p" connections (peer to peer, piazza to
piazza, plaza to plaza, etc…)  between the 8th and the 10th of July, in
coincidence of the date of the past G8 2009, link:


http://www.g8italia2009.it/G8/G8-G8_Layout_locale-1199882116809_Home.htm

http://www.g8italia2009.it/G8/Home/Summit/G8-G8_Layout_locale-1199882116809_Atti.htm

http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/G8_dell'Aquila<http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/G8_dell>


There is no so much time, but the web is the only weapon that allows to
collect and web-cast information "bottom up" …. cultural cooperation
together with an innovative use of  web 2.0 tools can help and we can
achieve our goal of a 1st wikiTV Festival ... in 2011 the 2nd edition will
be improved.


Please feel free to make a correction, comment, addition or a change on the
wiki discussion page just drafted as a first attempt to have an idea of the
general context in which to develop this initiative with the cultural
cooperation of international communities.

**
