 *MEOH aims at being a cooperative society whose purpose is to provide fund
raising and decision making tools for communities willing to achieve
projects in the creative domain. Its success will depend on people's will,
imagination and ability to collaborate.*

*Projects can be either social, entrepreneurial, educational, environmental,
humanitarian or in the artistic, creative and inventive domain... Projects
may originate from the civil, public or private sectors.*
