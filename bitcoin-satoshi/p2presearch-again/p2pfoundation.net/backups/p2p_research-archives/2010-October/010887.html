Dear Michel,

If you receive this letter, this means that you have been in contact with
Michel Bauwens or another associate of the P2P Foundation, and you may be
familiar with our work. For the first five years, we have been diligently
working on creating a knowledge commons, but we are now attempting to make
our efforts sustainable in the long run, in part by the creation of
knowledge products that will help people in the transition, plus possibly
other activities based on feedback from the community. Part of this effort
is a creation of a mailing list of our contacts, which we will never sell to
third parties and not abuse by too frequent communication, we are thinking
of maximum 6 communications per year, likely in the form of a summary
newsletter. Please tell us if you do not want to receive such messages in
the future, by clicking on the opt out link below, We really appreciate, and
need, your support for the P2P work we have been doing, and will continue to
undertake in the future,

Michel Bauwens, for the P2P Foundation
