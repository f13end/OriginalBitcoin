" “Why buy a drill if what you really want is a hole?” Alex told Nora about
product service systems, which turn products (like drills) into services
(like a drill rental service). There are many examples of product service
systems all around us. Car sharing services and public libraries are good
examples.

Now, there’s a new twist to some product service systems: peer-to-peer.
Instead of borrowing or renting from a company or organization, sites like
NeighborGoods and SnapGoods encourage their users to borrow from one
another.

To learn more about this trend, Nora talked to Micki Krimmel, founder of
NeighborGoods." (
http://www.cbc.ca/spark/2010/09/full-interview-micki-krimmel-on-neighborgoods/)
