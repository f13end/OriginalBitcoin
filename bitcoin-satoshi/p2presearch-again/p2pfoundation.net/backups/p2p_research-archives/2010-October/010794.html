Dear Massimo,

here are details about the conference:

not much of a link and conference may be private, see
http://www.cnpds.it/eng/index.php

Conference entitled The Effects of New Communication Technologies upon
Information, which will take place in Milan on October 22 -23, 2010.

The Fondazione Centro nazionale di prevenzione e difesa sociale was founded
in 1948 by my father, Adolfo Beria di Argentine, who was a jurist and
Attorney General in Milan, as a study and research centre focusing on social
sciences and on justice-related issues.
The Fondazione CNPDS coordinates research projects, organizes conferences
and publishes the proceedings.
The activities of Fondazione CNPDS are financed by the Italian Ministry of
Justice, the Region of Lombardy and numerous public and private bodies.
The above-mentioned Conference will be financed by the Fondazione Cariplo
which has a majority equity stake in Banca Intesa, Italy's largest bank.
