are you aware of this: http://www.openworld.com/

To speed up the development of "builder" (aka maker) efforts, the non-profit
venture fund OpenWorld <http://www.openworld.com/> has set up two sites.
 The first is "Builder Challenges" which is essentially a financial support
network using crowd sourcing to fund and support grassroots development
efforts.  The second is the Buildership Lab
<http://buildership.ning.com/>(a platform for start-ups in the space
akin to what we saw in the venture
capital space a decade ago).
