Michael H Goldhaber commented on your link:

"I think disenchantment with nature started much earlier. Medieval and
earlier cities were walled partly to keep nature, such as large carnivores
out. Nature, un-channeled was extremely dangerous, but hardly an
enchantment. Landscape painting came into vogue only about the time
industrialism did. The same goes for  the nature poets such as Wordsworth. I
think Walt Disney's nature documentaries had much to do with the
"re-enchantment.""
