Paola,

If you are interested I could certainly do something that is a convergence
of the following:

   - The notion of ecosystems as the appropriate organizing principle in a
   networked world
   - The requirement for alternative funding models if we want to build
   infrastructure to support social entrepreneurs
   - The notion of ecosystems as an organizational principle for a funding
   structure that can mitigate financial risk for social investors
   - The specific case of mapping the Distributed Manufacturing ecosystem.
   - Also, from a related by somewhat different perspective I can
   demonstrate how an ecosystem can serve to provide the right model for
   intra-organizational collaboration that provides organizations to scale
   their set of global relationships etc.

I am also wondering if you provide funding to conference speakers.

Regards,

Suresh
