Organizer Message
==========
I’m exceptionally keen to announce that planning for the Emerging
Communications Conference & Awards is now fully underway.

The website is now live (see http://america.eComm.ec) and the call for
speakers is now out (see
http://america.ecomm.ec/2010/uploads/2010_Call_for_Speakers_Alpha.pdf).

I’m terribly excited about the communications (Telecom, Mobile and Internet)
industry in 2010. eComm was the first event to cover both the iPhone and
Android back at the debut in 2008, 2009 (Europe) heavily featured the Google
Wave team and I have very good reason to believe that America 2010 is going
to be a monumental year. Telecoms will not be viewed the same when 2010
closes.

You’ll find the list of trends/topics on both the website and in the call
for speakers.

I look forward to your participation!
