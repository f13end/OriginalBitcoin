As announced earlier, the old P2P Research mailman list is being shut
down because of problems with the  host.

A successor list has been created.  You can subscribe here:
http://lists.ourproject.org/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/p2p-foundation

It is a mailman list like the old one, and subscribing is very fast and easy.

If you wish to continue participating on the list, please click the
link and subscribe immediately.  It won't take two minutes.

We need to stop having ongoing discussion threads on two lists at
once, and complete the migration as soon as possible.  So...

Please stop posting responses to threads on the old list, effective
immediately.  If you respond to a message from the old list, please
forward your response to the new list after you have subscribed to it.
