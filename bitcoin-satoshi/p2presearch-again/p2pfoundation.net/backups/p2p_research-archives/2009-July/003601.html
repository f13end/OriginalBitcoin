*Dear Panel

Please find below and attached this month’s new materials list.
Please let Bryony know at **ldr at leonardo.org**  what would like to review
and we will do our best to get the materials to you as soon as possible.

A note on biographies: We are gradually changing the way that we publish the
panel biographies. Hannah is working on this but the main change will be
that your bio will be linked to your review and we will phase out the
dedicated bio page (It will be replaced by a list of panel members.)

The reason for this is that the bio page is rather uneven – some panel
members do not keep it up to date  etc so it has a negative effect on those
who do. It also means that you can update your bio from time to time when
you send in a review.

I hope you agree this adds currency and clarity to the page.

All good wishes
Michael
