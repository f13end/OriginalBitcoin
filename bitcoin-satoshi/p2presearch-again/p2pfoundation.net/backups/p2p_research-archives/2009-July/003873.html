> OpenKollab.  This message will give you some idea about it, but the
> documentation at http://openkollab.com breaks it down more.  We're having
> our weekly open chat session in Skype (email me your Skype name for an

> over at OpenKollab.  I'm really excited about the group that's gathered
> around this, and the degree of focus we've achieved.
>
> Best,
> Matt

> ---------------
>
> WHATS HAPPENING WITH OPENKOLLAB
> The follow up to Pcamp (http://mudball.net/pcamp09/) has now resulted
> in the formation of a team (Matt, Tav, Michael, Stephan, Gerry and
> Suresh) that are working on developing an initiative called
> OpenKollab. OpenKollab Project will help organizations that are
> strategically aligned explore potential working relationships. It will
> do this by identifying groups with related goals, highlighting
> opportunities for resource sharing between those groups, and making it
> easy for those groups to hold a productive virtual meeting. OpenKollab
> is both a community process and a platform. While you all are invited
> to take part in building the community and thinking through the
> platform needs, Tav and his crew of Espians - a group of visionary
> technologists that are working on developing a next generation
> architecture to support distributed real time collaboration and
> community formation - have agreed to build parts of the platform.
>
>
> AGENDA FOR WEDNESDAYS MEETING
> This Wednesday, we are going to focus our discussion on:
> -The OpenKollab Platform: What do our web services do now, and what
> will they do in the future?
> -The Roadmap: What is our longer term plan?
> -Project Mapping: How will we gather the necessary information to map
> projects and realte them to each other?
>
>
> OUR UNIQUE SYSTEM FOR RELATING PROJECTS
> Our next project is to create a standard format for structuring
> information about projects.  Once projects are listed in this format,
> it's easier to see how they fit together.
>
> The OpenKollab Project's own listing looks like this:
> ·       Needs: project mappers, funding support,
> ·       Resources: a place to list your project, share it with a community,
> and hold a virtual meeting
> ·       Members: mattcoop, tav, tropology, gerryg, sureshf, sdohrn
> ·       Super-Ordinate Goal(s): stimulating collaboration amongst related
> projects
> ·       Sub-Ordinate Goals(s): getting you to attend our meeting, etc.
>
> Details on the technology specifications, the roadmap as well as the
> mapping process can be found at:
