continue. I am quite ciritcal of so-called "Social Media," and see it as an

goals of the venture communist project telekommunisten we must exploit all
possible avenues of building our community and support for worker's controlledi
enterprises and worker's control of production broadly, and so therefore, 
must communicate where the people are, not where we think they should be.
