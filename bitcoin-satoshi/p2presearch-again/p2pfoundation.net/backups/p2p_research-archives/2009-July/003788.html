Not that it would by any means be a surprise to folks on this list, but
 this paper
 <http://academiccommons.columbia.edu:8080/ac/bitstream/10022/AC:P:29800/1/STLR-Vol10-Torrance.pdf>
 discusses an on-line simulation of alternate patent systems (traditional,
 none and hybrid) which suggests that innovation, productivity and societal
 utility are greater when there is no patent protection.
