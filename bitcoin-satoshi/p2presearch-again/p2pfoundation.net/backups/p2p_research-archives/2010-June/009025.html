Hi all,

The Free Technology Academy (FTA) <http://ftacademy.org/> seeks to
write about (or write up) a few case studies as part of an educational
programme on Free Software and Open Standards.

Three case studies will be selected, adapted and published (CC-BY-SA)
among the learning materials illustrating real world experiences.

If you know of any good free software case studies which could be used
or adapted, especially if they concern NGOs adopting/migrating to
free/libre and open source software, please list them on WikiVersity
here:

http://en.wikiversity.org/wiki/Free_Software_Case_Studies

or WikiEducator (login required) here:

http://wikieducator.org/Free_Software_Case_Studies/Case_Studies

The FTA will select from these and other lists.
