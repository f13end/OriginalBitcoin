E+SM  Presents SynchLink<http://www.realitysandwich.com/sites/realitysandwich.civicactions.net/modules/civicrm/extern/url.php?u=5443&qid=651734>



This week we are delighted to introduce a new, FREE service that will give
you the power to meet, chat, and build connections - at any time - with
members of the global transformational community.

Have you ever longed for the chance to discuss gardening, activism,
shamanism, the Archaic Revival or the Technological Singularity, but there
were no Evolvers at hand?

Have you ever dreamed of honing your spontaneous haiku or performance-art
skills with charming strangers who share alternative visions of what this
world is, and could be?

The Evolver Social Movement launches
SynchLink<http://www.realitysandwich.com/sites/realitysandwich.civicactions.net/modules/civicrm/extern/url.php?u=5443&qid=651734>,
tailoring the enormously popular social media site ChatRoulette, to enhance
our  to our community of global Evolvers, Zeitgeisters, TransitionTowners,
Integralites, GreenBusniks, and whoever else chooses to come on board.
ChatRoulette attracts tens of millions of users per month, but if you’ve
tried it, you’ve likely noticed a lack of deep discussion or good humored
rapport, and a plethora of men revealing parts better left private! But it’s
not the tool, it’s how you use it. In your hands, used by a community that
shares interests and seeks new inspirations and ideas, we can use it to
accelerate the transformation of human consciousness – and have fun making
new friends at the same time.  Hence we invite you to try SynchLink as a
social experiment and a new way to "find the others" so we can co-create
global community.

After trying SynchLink, please leave a comment on this blog
post<http://www.realitysandwich.com/sites/realitysandwich.civicactions.net/modules/civicrm/extern/url.php?u=5445&qid=651734>and
discuss your experiences, ideas for improvement, and share stories of
your meetings and random-access exchanges. The technology is in beta, so
some browsers work better than others, the sound sometimes reverbs and the
image sometimes disappears. Please experiment and explore, but for now there
is little we can do to change the system.

Evolver Social Movement  is providing this for free, but if you value the
effort we make to bring you new experiences and ideas, please join up
<http://www.realitysandwich.com/sites/realitysandwich.civicactions.net/modules/civicrm/extern/url.php?u=5447&qid=651734>and
become a contributing member so we can develop more fun projects and our
global community of conscious activists working together to create a better
world.

SynchLink
<http://www.realitysandwich.com/sites/realitysandwich.civicactions.net/modules/civicrm/extern/url.php?u=5443&qid=651734>is
always on, but we thought we'd set up the following convening times (or
"magic hours") to click connect and encourage a new, possibly more intuitive
way of gathering online:

*Sunday, June 20th, 9PM EST*

*Tuesday, June 22nd, 2PM EST
*

*Tuesday, June 22nd, 9PM EST*

Please spread the word and make a note of these times and have your web
camera ready!
