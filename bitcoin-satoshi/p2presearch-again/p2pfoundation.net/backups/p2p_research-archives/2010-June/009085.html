> I just launched a kickstarter campaign for my big idea: a new collaborative
> environment for stock media (stock media = stock photography, brushes,
> vectors, videos, audio, etc).
>
> My site, casually named "Stocky," will be a community effort that
> encourages users to collaborate and share their work, accessing other's work
> in return. Sharing will be the name of the game, taking influence from the
> open source community. Many designers and artists already share some of
> their work on their own sites, and Stocky is here to unite them.
>
> I have a video, a campaign site and rewards to try and get things going,
> but I am wondering if there's any way you'd like to help me out by writing
> about it?
>
> Thanks so much for reading,
> Harrison
> Site Link: http://kck.st/cAQ3wP
>
> WHY HELP?
> A new take on stock media can help the design, art and small business
> community! I see the current stock media situation as a dried up well. It
> has a some great work and a lot of junk being sold, abused, stolen and
> recycled, roundup after roundup. Furthermore, owners heavily protect work
> that is meant to be given away. Protecting your property is very important,
> but why give half way? With your help, Stocky will be a new place to
> cultivate artwork and designs that can help others in their own endeavors.
