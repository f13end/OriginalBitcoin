This is exactly the sort of idea where P2P's real impact will next blossom
IMO...this could be done for clothing, used furniture and new, for health
services...for pharmacy...for childcare, youth programmes, sports leagues,
for schools...for universities...for banks...(we used to call them building
societies)...  Some people have been referring to these as secular
churches.  Of course it is just effective civil society.

As an aside, I have a relation using Zip and City Cars in London...extremely
effective and very positive review..."best idea ever" was their response.
