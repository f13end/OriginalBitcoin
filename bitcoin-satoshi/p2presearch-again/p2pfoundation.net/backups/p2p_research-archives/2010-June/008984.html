Great progress!

Cheers to all the folks who worked on the Open Business models sections.
 Those are entries to some of the riches treasures on the wiki.

There are a number of 'supporting concepts' that are linked out from these
pages, commonalities, that I hope folks can glance at when they get a
chance, like: transparency, replicability, and scale invariance.

These concepts are so new that even the wikipedia descriptions leave a lot
to be desired, and our pages offer more context and application examples
than wikipedia ever could.  I urge folks to try on the 'supporting concepts'
perspective, or whatever you want to call it, to see if our wiki pages are
robust enough for your taste.
