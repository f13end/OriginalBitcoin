Dear Alex,



Sorry for the delay in answering your question.

My proposal is the following. You could help in making researchers who are
investigating peer to peer topics in a real community of knowledge exchange,
with the aim of ultimately having some kind of organization like the asssn.
of internet researchers.

But the first step is mapping, i.e. finding out who is researching in this
area, and to add their names, bios, and research interests in our Research
category.

We should have some kind of template for this, and then ask them by email.
For each country we should have  a number of names. At the same time, we
should compile what we have already done, and also add to those entries.

This should take about 6 months, and at that time we can discuss further
steps.

In the meantime, you could use the facebook group created by smari to
mention the most interesting material that you are finding while doing this,
and perhaps report once a month on our blog as well.

What do you think?

We can start with updating the material we already have …
