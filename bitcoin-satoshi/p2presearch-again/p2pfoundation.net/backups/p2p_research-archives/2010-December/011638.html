ENGLISH :
RemixTheCommons (http://www.remixthecommons.org/) a digital collaborative
project working on commons, has done its first video on the occasion of
international conferences held in Berlin in November by the Heinrich Boell
Foundation and the Commons International Strategic Group (IIC). 14 videos
Commons on Vimeo (http://www.vimeo.com/user5449180/videos) and soon on other
channels (youtube, blip.tv and dailymotion).

Alain Ambrosi, Simon Roux From COMMUNAUTIQUE, with the help of Abeille Tard
and Javier Moya, make available interviews of some participants in The
Berlin Conference and three shorts films (1, 3 and 16 minutes) of
definitions of Commons offered in the many languages of the participants.
The diversity of languages is a part of the definitions theirself!

RemixTheCommons aims to document and illustrate the ideas and practices
surrounding the commons paradigm while explaining the process directly by
the collective creation. The project is a cooperative and iterative process.
It will be fully operational in a few months.

The team RemixTheCommons will be present in Dakar to capture meetings and
debates on Commons.

Meanwhile, these videos are accessible and usable by all. You can put them
on your own sites, distribute and of course ... remix !
