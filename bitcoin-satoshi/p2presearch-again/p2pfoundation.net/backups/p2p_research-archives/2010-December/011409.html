Dear commoner-friends,

yesterday night I watched the premiere of Ralf Schmerbergs new film
"Problema" in Berlin at the "Volksbühne". It's a 90 minutes documentary on
the "Table of Free Vvoices" having taken place on the August-Bebel-Platz in
2006 with 112 speakers responding simultaneoulsy to 100 questions collected
in a one years commoning-process. Among the speakers are political
activists, scientists and artists from all over the world concerned about a
peaceful future. Ralf clothed the commens of the speakers in a most
impressive collage of on the one hand horrible scences of war and
destruction and on the other hand most beautiful images of nature and
artworks. I'ts an invitation to confront yourself once again with the
unadorned reality of colonialism and exploitation and to struggle to find
bits and pieces of a new narrative for the future telling different stories.
There are some specially beautiful and inspiring bits of such narratives in
many of the answers of the thinkers at the table.

"Problema" is a mindpirates production, so the film is free for download and
may be showed also publicly without asking for permission.

http://www.problema-thefilm.org

Maybe you find the time to look at it and spread the link to this new common
good.

All the best,
