Hi all,

A big thank you to everyone who attended the Open Government Data Camp
2010 last month. We've just put up a page for all video, photos,
coverage and other material from the event. If there's anything you
think we should add, please don't hesitate to email ogdcamp at okfn.org
and let us know!

 <http://opengovernmentdata.org/camp2010/after/>

We're also going to add everyone who came to a new (very low volume!)
ogdcamp-announce list, to keep everyone informed about the next event.
If you would prefer not to be added please let us know!

You may also be interested in:

 * The Eurostat Hackday on Wednesday 16th December 2010:
<http://eurostat.okfn.org/>
 * The international open-government mailing list: <http://bit.ly/ogd-list>
 * The next annual Open Knowledge Conference, OKCon 2011
<http://okfn.org/okcon/>

If you enjoyed the event please consider supporting the Open Knowledge
Foundation <http://okfn.org/support/>. Your support helps us develop
and run events like this (and much more).

Regards,

Rufus Pollock, Jonathan Gray and the OGD Camp team.
