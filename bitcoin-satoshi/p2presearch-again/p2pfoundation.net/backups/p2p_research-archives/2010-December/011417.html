Launched to mark the start of bank bonus season, a new animation is setting
out to increase public pressure on government to take on the banks and not
sweep reform under the carpet. It ask politicians whether they have a plan
to tame the bank, and if not, why not?

The minute-long animation is inspired by *Rolling Stone *journalist Matt
Taibbi's description of investment bank Goldman Sachs as a giant vampire
squid "sucking on the face of humanity".

The animation is backed by a wide range of influential pressure groups
including: *nef*, Compass, PLATFORM, ResPublica, 38 Degrees, WDM, Positive
Money, Tax Research and the Post Bank campaign.
