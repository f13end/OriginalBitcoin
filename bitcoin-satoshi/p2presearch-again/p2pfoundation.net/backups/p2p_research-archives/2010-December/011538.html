Yahoo Says Delicious For Sale, Blames Press for Confusion

   - By Sam Gustin <http://www.wired.com/epicenter/author/sam_gustin/> [image:
   Email Author]  <shgustin at gmail.com>

excerpted :

“While we have determined that there is not a strategic fit at Yahoo, we
believe there is a ideal home for Delicious outside of the company where it
can be resourced to the level where it can be competitive,” the company said
in a blog post<http://blog.delicious.com/blog/2010/12/whats-next-for-delicious.html>
.

In other words, the company apparently does care enough about Delicious to
invest in it at “the level where it can be competitive.”
