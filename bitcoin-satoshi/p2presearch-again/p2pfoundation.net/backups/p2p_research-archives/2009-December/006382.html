Dear Panel

Hannah Drayson one of the associate editors who looks after the website has
been steadily beavering away refining it. She is just linking search engines
on each page which I think is a great asset. We are also thinking quite hard
about how to manage the bios. Many are missing and many out of date and I
think we should not have that page. I wonder whether the best solution is a
two/three line bio at the end of every review you file with a link to your
url  thoughts on this would be welcome.

Other developments are the we are now about to prepare the first  six
monthly Leonardo Reviews Digest which will be available as a PDF and print
on demand and allow us to extend the reviews that are printed. We hope after
the first dummy that each Digest will be headed by an overview by one of the
panel  more on this in the new year.



Attached and below is the list of new material available for this month. As
you see its an extensive list again with some rich new material.
I hope now that we have stabilised the email problems which seemed to make
life a little difficult over the last few months.

Please send your requests to Bryony at ldr at leonardo.info and we will get
them to you as soon as possible.

Thank you for your support for this project.

All good wishes

Michael
