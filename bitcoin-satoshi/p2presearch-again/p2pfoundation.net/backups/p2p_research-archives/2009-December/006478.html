G'day Michel !

You may remember the Social Media Report which I passed on to you in June
this year.

I'm pleased to let you know that we have now publicly released Volume 2 of
the report, which looks more closely at online user communities and presents
a range of recommendations for engaging with them. It's aimed at
organisations and businesses which want to work with social media
communities, but so far don't have a firm understanding about how to do so
(and there are still too many organisations which are in that situation...).

I'm attaching the document here - please feel free to pass it on to any
contacts who may be interested. Any feedback would be much appreciated, of
course.
