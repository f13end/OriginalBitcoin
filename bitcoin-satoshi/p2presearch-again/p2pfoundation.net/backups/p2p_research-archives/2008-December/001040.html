Dear IASC member

The deadline for submitting to the 15th Annual International Sustainable
Development Research Conference (Utrecht, The Netherlands,  July 5-8th,
2009) is now approaching.

Track nr 5A: Inducing self-governance for sustainable development:
Evaluating and comparing New Environmental Policy Instruments and Common
Pool Resources Management is devoted to decentralized governance and
common-pool resource management. A full description of the track is
attached.  See also  http://globalchallenge2009.geo.uu.nl .

The conference will provide a very exiting platform and context for global
sustainable development scientific research, policy and practice. The
central theme of the conference is "Taking Up the Global Challenge:
Analysing the implementation of innovations and governance for sustainable
development".

Scholars from all over the world are invited to submit their abstract before
January 1st, 2009.
