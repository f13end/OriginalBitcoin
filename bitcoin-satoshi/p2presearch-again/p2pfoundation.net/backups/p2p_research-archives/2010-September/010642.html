Now let's "*Share It Forward*"...  we are building a national movement to
share our wares and wear our shares!

*It's Simple!  Here's how it works...*

*(1)* List one item or local service to be shared or rented on
www.rentalic.com right now.  If you don't have 3 pictures of the item right
now, don't worry, you can add pictures later.  Just add 1 item to share or
rent *within the next ten minutes*.  It could be something as simple as a
book, costume, tool, DVD or a video game.

*(2)* Add your first name and city to the list at the bottom of this email,
copy and paste your item link next to your name.

*(3)* Forward this email to 5 of your friends, neighbors, family, or
colleagues and encourage them to *Share It Forward.*

Now watch how sharing grows lightening fast due to your contribution!  Don't
wait any longer to be a part of this growing national movement.  Right now
is the best time to participate!
