I find this quite fascinating:

see http://deugarte.com/phyles, but especially the longer essay/book here at
http://deugarte.com/gomi/phyles.pdf (own summary at

This economic structure of phyles, is a key missing element in p2p theory
(though I was groping for it in my own modelling of the economy of the

using in my own synthesis.
