the symposium is organised by the PhD program Planetary Collegium M-Node, as
director I would pointing out the "state of the art" of higher education in
the field of Art & Media, Media Theories, Ecologies of Media, for that there
will be a bunch of theoretician, artist, videomakers, researchers as Roy
Ascott, Antonio Caronia, Pierluigi Capucci, Derrick De Kerckhove, Denis
Jaromil Rojo, Amos Bianchi, Tommaso Tozzi, Paolo Rosa, Alterazioni Video,
Sarah Ciracì....

Celebrating the 30 years of the Nuova Accademia delle Belle Arti - New
Academy of Fine Arts in Milan, NABA <http://www.naba.it/>, and as part of
our blueprint program aimed at exploring the relations among culture, art
and technologies, the PhD Program of the Planetary
Collegium<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planetary_Collegium>’s
M-Node <http://www.m-node.org/> and the NABA School of Media Design & New
Media Art, in collaboration with *NoemaLab* <http://www.noemalab.org/> are
organizing a 3-day meeting on December 16 – 18, 2010, in Milan @NABA to
challenge, foresee and debate in a subversive, non linear, anarchic and
(non) academic free way, future strategies and forms of education in Media,
Art & Design and the role of the research and production spaces for artists
and creative professionals working with technologies.

The tile of the symposium is “*Always Already New - Thinking Media,
Subversing Feeling, Scaffolding Knowledge: Art and Education in the Praxis
of Transformation* ” and is part of the *New Media Art Education &
Research*conferences that the PhD Program M-Node and
the NABA Department of Media Design & New Media Art have been organizing for
a few years, see here. <http://www.m-node.org/mnode_conferences.html> The
education and research programs will provide opportunities for discussion
and engagement with a wide range of professionals: teachers, researchers,
scholars, curators, meetings’ experts, from art and technology labs across
Italy and worlwide.

After the three days with international participants from many realms
NABAMedia Design & New Media Arts and M-NODE
in collaboration with *NoemaLab* will also identify the key topics for a
follow-up expert’s meeting as well as some urgent research areas to focus on
in the 2011 symposium.

The symposium will produce a pubblication an a map.


more on neomalab draft site: http://www.noemalab.org/always_already_new
