Activist <http://www.eff.org/about/opportunities/activist>

The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), a nonprofit organization based in
San Francisco's Mission District, is looking for a full-time activist to
fight for free speech, innovation and privacy on the Internet and other
technologies. If you are passionate about technology, dedicated to its role
in building a better future and want to work alongside leaders in the field,
this dream job is for you! As an EFF activist, your responsibilities would
include:

   - Communicating our sometimes complex technical and legal issues to "mom
   and pop" in a clear, compelling and concise voice;
   - Developing energetic and innovative educational, fundraising and
   activism campaigns that engage both EFF's membership and the general public;
   - Writing blog posts about EFF work in a timely manner;
   - Maintaining the content of EFF's information-packed website;
   - Working closely with EFF attorneys and other public interest groups;
   and
   - Determining when issues deserve EFF attention and convincing others of
   their importance.

Required:

   - Excellent writing skills;
   - Experience maintaining and contributing to a blog;
   - Strong familiarity with digital civil liberties issues;
   - An eagerness to learn about new issues; and
   - An ability to turn things around quickly and comprehensively.

In addition, knowledge of or experience with any of the following would be
advantageous:

   - Collaborative writing and editing;
   - Organizing online activism for an advocacy-oriented nonprofit
   organization;
   - Technical skills, including content management systems;
   - International policy issues; and
   - Design and/or illustration.

This is a full-time position based in EFF's office in San Francisco, CA. To
apply, send a cover letter, writing sample, and resume to activist at eff.org.
Feel free to include links to additional supporting materials, as well. No
phone calls please!
