littleBits are tiny circuitboards that you can stick together with
magnets to make little electronic machines.

Just as Legos allow you to create complex structures with very little
engineering knowledge, littleBits are simple, intuitive,
space-sensitive blocks that make prototyping with sophisticated
electronics a matter of snapping small magnets together. With a growing
number of available modules, littleBits aims to move electronics from
late stages of the design process to its earliest ones, and from the
hands of experts, to those of artists, makers and designers.

A bit like Siftables.
