Dante,

It's really about bringing the intuitive and the conscious together.

For example, the measure of wholeness (of the dynamism) can be expressed as
the degree of alignment between the intuitive feeling and the conscious
expression.

So you can say something (in text) to express thoughts consciously and play
something (with music) to intuit a feeling. If the combined message
resonates as an expression of a dynamic whole.

We can experiment with such communication tools. I use FB for it now in a
rather unstructured, non-obvious way but we could build a dedicated FB app
for this kind of interactivity

Marc
