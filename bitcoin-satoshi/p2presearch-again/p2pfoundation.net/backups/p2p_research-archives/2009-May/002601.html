  *7 Key R2 Innovation Crucibles*

[image: Image]The R2 events will deliver real outcomes by interweaving the 7
Key R2 Innovation Crucibles. The first four innovation crucibles, *Renewable
energy*, *Resilient Environments*, *Enlightened Enterprise* and *Integral
Governance* are inspired and fueled by the accelerated evolution of human
consciousness. The three “soft” crucibles *Conscious Evolution*, *Human
Wellbeing* and *Wise Culture* measure the individual and organizational
stages of human development.

There are already 12 great speakers lined up for the Great Shift Partner

includes Green Cross president *Michael Gorbachev* and musician *Peter
Gabriel*.
