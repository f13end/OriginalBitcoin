I should rephrase the question as:

Why do we seek to have scarcity (or rarity, uniqueness) when it comes to
physical objects?

Why are we happier when the piece of antique furniture we find is the last
surviving piece on the planet and is now ours? And why would people still go
see ORIGINAL Picasso paintings even if they could be copied down to the
atomic scale and distributed freely?

What is it in our nature that causes us to seek such uniqueness in objects
(but not in digital objects or information)

...?

Hmm.
