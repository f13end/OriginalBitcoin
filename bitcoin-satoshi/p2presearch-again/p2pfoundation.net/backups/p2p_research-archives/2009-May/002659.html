FIRE and Living Labs - Future Internet by the people

The conference “FIRE and Living Labs – Future Internet by the people” is
held in association with the Swedish EU presidency in autumn 2009 where two
strong communities will meet and exchange experiences: The FIRE community
focusing on Future Internet Research and Experimentation and the Living Labs
community with the mission to engage people, users as co-creators in the
development of new successful products and services.

We expect attendance to be 300+ delegates of experts mainly European,
academics, industry from ongoing projects and authorities, European
Commission and international organisations.
