As you can see, the aim of this workshop is to study the future of
industrial design within the information society, and we're going to draw
three maps of these aspects: needs, disruptive solutions, existing answers.
We are interested in open design, generative design, fabbing,
mass-customization, open p2p marketplaces, ...
