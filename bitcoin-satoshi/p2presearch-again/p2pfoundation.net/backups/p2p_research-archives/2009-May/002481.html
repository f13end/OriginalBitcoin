I've been thinking about freecycling lately.  Some of the things you are
saying remind me of freecycling.

Changing property systems isn't going to resolve the tensions of waste or
excess; it is only going to alienate opposing power.  I feel that getting
others to transform an ethos is a slow, challenging and ultimately humbling
process that is necessary to remake societies.

One person at a time, we change by entering into dialogues about
possibilities and futures.  Sometimes it is frustrating.  Sometimes it is a
failure.  In the larger picture, I feel it is the only way.


Ryan Lanham
