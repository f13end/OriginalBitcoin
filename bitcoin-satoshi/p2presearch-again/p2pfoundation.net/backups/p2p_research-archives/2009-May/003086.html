Hi and welcome to Imindi, a thought engine to help you to organize your own
thoughts and explore the thoughts of like-minded people.

You would use Imindi is that when you combine the thoughts in your mind with
the thoughts of people who have already been thinking about similar things,
you discover knowledge and insights that you may never have considered on
your own.

Thank you for choosing Imindi, its very early days and we know we have a lot
of rough edges and a lot to learn from all of you which is why this is a
private Beta and not a public launch. Please don`t hesitate to contact me at
adlin at imindi.com with any questions about Imindi.
