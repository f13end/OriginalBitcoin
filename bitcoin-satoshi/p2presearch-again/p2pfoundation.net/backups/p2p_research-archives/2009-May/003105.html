Dear All,

The Centre for Internet and Society, Bangalore, is conducting a review
of Youth Technology, political participation and Social Change. The
review includes existing scholarship, case-studies, projects and
movements in different parts of the world, but especially in the global
south, with a special emphasis on Digital Natives. We are looking for
projects, initiatives or movements that are in the field of technology
(internet, cell-phones, etc.) and focussed on one or many of the
following areas:

1. Youth and political participation

2. Youth and social transformation

3. Youth and livelihood

4. Youth and cultural production/consumption.

If you could please send us links to people or projects in your region
that you think might fit our review and help us in consolidating a
comprehensive list of areas to support, engage with and collaborate
with, it would be of the greatest help.

Warmly
