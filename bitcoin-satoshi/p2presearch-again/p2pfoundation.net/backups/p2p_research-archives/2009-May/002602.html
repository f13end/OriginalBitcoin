Coherence of a written message used to be a function of its linguistic
structure, logic and clarity, with length playing a small part.

In the age if the Internet, where we are swimming in an ocean of
messages, the coherence of (or our ability to parse) a written message
is becoming predominantly a function of its length.

This is also called ADHD-favorable communication.

So all the ADHD people out there, ones who are most likely unaware of
their condition, have favored Twitter.

Twitter is really the voice of the ADHD generation.

Just stating the obvious....
