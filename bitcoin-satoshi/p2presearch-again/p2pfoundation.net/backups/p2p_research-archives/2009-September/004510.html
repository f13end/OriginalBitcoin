http://www.open-innovation-projects.org/projects/show/172/clustic

Clustic is a social network, developed with TOG an extensible open source
platform for social networks, that fosters interaction among companies,
researcher communities and investors to develop and share R+D projects,
ideas and funds, to set up a globally connected network. The aim of Clustic
is to create a space where these communities can participate and be in
closer contact. To achieve this goal, all of the participants would interact
choosing their role or roles: Researcher, Company or Investor.
