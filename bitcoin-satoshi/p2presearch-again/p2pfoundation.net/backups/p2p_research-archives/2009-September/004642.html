Dear Friends of Imindi,

Several months ago, you signed up for an account to join the private beta
test for Imindi. Thank you so much.

Over the last few months, we learnt many things from you all and built some
great new features to make Imindi much more useful and enjoyable.

Basically, we learnt that many of you care deeply about various topics in
the world and in your lives and want a way to share your thoughts, opinions
and insights about these topics with others who might care about the same
things.

In short, you told us that you wanted to connect with like-minded people in
a familiar discussion format. We have built this.

You can still collect your thoughts and file them into appropriate
categories or Think Tanks like Home and Family or Art and Creativity, but
what is great is that other people can easily contribute to these thoughts
by adding their thoughts in a discussion thread.

People who have joined the same Think Tanks as you may serendipitously
discover your thoughts in real-time or even many months later via our index
of related thoughts. Either way, Imindi will help you to connect with
like-minded people.

Imindi is built on sophisticated technology but we did not build our service
because we care about technology, but because we care about helping people
like you to make meaningful connections by sharing your thoughts and ideas
with other like-minded people.

You can log-in to Imindi or sign in again to Imindi at http://www.imindi.com.
We look forward to seeing you there. If you have forgotten your password, go
to http://www.imindi.com/login and enter your email in the "Lost your
password?" box.

Please send any comments or suggestions about Imindi to adlin at imindi.com or
post a discussion in the Innovation and Technology Think Tank.

Thank you,

Adam Lindemann
Founder
Imindi Inc
