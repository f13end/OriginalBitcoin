Dear Madam, Sir,



Since you have, in the past, shown interest in the Socio-economic Sciences
and Humanities (SSH) programme, we would like to inform you about the new
Work Programme 2010,  which was published on 30 July 2009.





In the four calls for proposals a large variety of funding schemes are
available, from large collaborative projects addressing major societal
challenges, through traditional focused research projects and supporting
actions to specifically targeted initiatives such as specific international
cooperation actions or research for the benefit of civil society
organisations.





Deadline for receipt of the proposals for the first three calls is *2
February 2010 at 17:00, Brussels local time*:

   - SSH-2010-1 - Collaborative projects (large scale integrating research
   projects)
   - SSH-2010-2 - Collaborative projects (small or medium-scale focused
   research projects)
   - SSH-2010-3 - Collaborative projects (small or medium-scale focused
   research projects) for specific cooperation actions dedicated to
   international cooperation





Deadline for receipt of the proposals for the fourth call is *15 December
2009 at 17:00, Brussels local time*:

   - SSH-2010-4 - Coordination and support actions





All call information is accessible on a dedicated CORDIS
website<http://cordis.europa.eu/fp7/dc/index.cfm?fuseaction=UserSite.FP7ActivityCallsPage&id_activity=8>.
(
http://cordis.europa.eu/fp7/dc/index.cfm?fuseaction=UserSite.FP7ActivityCallsPage&id_activity=8
)





We would also like to invite you to visit a dedicated SSH
website<http://ec.europa.eu/research/social-sciences/index_en.html>
 (http://ec.europa.eu/research/social-sciences/index_en.html),  where you
will find a wealth of information on the programme, funded projects,
interesting events, as well as SSH publications.





Best regards,

SSH-2010 Call Coordination Team
