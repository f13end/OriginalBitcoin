Lee Dryburgh sent a message to the members of eComm.

--------------------
Subject: Alphabetical Listing of Talks Accepted so Far

Organiser Message
----------------
The is no other event spanning two continents with a fast-paced 15 minute
single track running for three days covering everything emerging in
communications across telecom, mobile and Internet. There is no other event
which was created to promote and accelerate communications innovation. There
is no other telecom event which does not allow sponsors to buy stage time
thereby cutting out "brochureware" and putting value instead on the time and
attention of each attendee.

As this event seeks to stay independent and serve the communications
innovation community, I'd appreciate it if you could let your colleagues and
friends know about the event. Together we can keep exposing exceptional
opportunity across the communications industry and we can work closely
together to seize it.

It's clearly not a nice to attend event, it's a must attend. As such I look
forward to seeing many of you there next month.

Regards, Lee S Dryburgh
