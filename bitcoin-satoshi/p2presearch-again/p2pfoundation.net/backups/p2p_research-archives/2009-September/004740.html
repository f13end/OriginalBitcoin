Dear R2 WorldShifters,

History was made at The British Museum on Wednesday *09/09/09* with the
launch of *WorldShift 2012*. The pictures of this event which launched the
Declaration of Global Emergency are now up. Just click on this
slideshow<http://picasaweb.google.co.uk/gsj.allmail/WorldShift2012GlobalLaunchBritishMuseum999?authkey=Gv1sRgCMKW0MfH0JqLugE#slideshow/5380637581368571346>to

The event was a resounding success with over 160 people from all corners of
the globe, joining together to "Shift the World" in response to the
Declaration. The next step in this process is the Great Shift
Gathering<http://www.renaissance2.eu/events/index.php>in Perpignan
from 22-24 October ("Designing a Resilient Civilization") and
25-25 October ("Conscious Evolution"), where we will also launch the
WorldShift Alliance.

the birth of the Worldshift Alliance, the movement that will bring together
that group of dedicated individuals and organizations which will form the
critical mass that can, and will, shift the world."
