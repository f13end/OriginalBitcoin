 Dear Michel,

I would like to bring to your attention the attached paper entitled
"Information technology tools for a transition economy". Here I suggest the
development of social networking tools that may facilitate the emergence of
local economic systems (local communities) that are not intrinsically
dependent on a global market economy, thus, facilitating the transition to a
sustainable society.

This work is in great resonance with your work at P2PFoundation.net. I hope
you find it useful. Please let me know if you have any comments as this is a
work in progress and I would like to hear as much criticisms, suggestions as
possible.

I hope the social cloud concept may grow into a big free software project
and someday become bundled with of most Linux distributions. I would like to
ask for your support in raising awareness of this ideas within the Free
Software Foundation.

You may also find this paper at
http://www.socialcloud.net/papers/ITtools.pdf .

Best Regards,
Rafael Pezzi
