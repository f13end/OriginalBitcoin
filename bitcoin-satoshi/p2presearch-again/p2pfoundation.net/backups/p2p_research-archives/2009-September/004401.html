> Hi Michel, I have a proposal for a small collaboration between P2P
> foundation and Telekommunisten.
>
> As you know I have been using and thinking about social media a lot lately.
> WRT to social news sites, such as hacker news, digg, etc,
>
> One problem with all these sites is that they have a pro-capitalist
> bias, especially pro-venture capital. They suffer from the "California
> Idiology." It would be great to have a social news site that served a
> more critical, more socially minded demographic. As links relating to
> social issues and political critique tend to get burried a the above
> sites.
>
> You have done great job with the P2P foundation of creating a community,
> and I think the P2P foundation should have such a site of it's own, for
> us to use in the way we use Hacker News.
>
> Telekommunisten would be happy to implement and administer such a system
> on behalf of the P2P foundation, if you think this is a good idea.
