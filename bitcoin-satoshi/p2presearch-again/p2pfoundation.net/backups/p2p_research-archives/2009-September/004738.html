Greetings!

Please find attached the flier for the forthcoming Media Ecologies and
Postindustrial Production day conference, at the Innovation Forum, near
University of Salford campus (made possible through a special alumni grant).


Distribute the poster any way you see fit... thank you!

Industrialisation started here in Manchester, and post-industrialisation is
an ongoing process. At this event we will discuss how we intend to promote
sustainable political economic and production models using all resources
available. Speakers will be talking about Fab Labs, radical ideas toward
collaborative platforms, and other peer to peer models of production that
defy  competition and the strictures of capitalistism. The event is
intended, furthermore, to launch the Peer to Peer Research Group, an
independent group linked to the Peer to Peer Foundation
http://p2pfoundation.net/The_Foundation_for_P2P_Alternatives

Contact me for any more details. There is no registration fee.
