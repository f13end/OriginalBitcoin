Hi All,

In case you haven't heard, OneWebDay happens every 9/22.  It's kinda like
Earth Day for the Internet.  This year's theme is Access for All, which is
appropriate for these lists since so many of our projects depend on access
to the web.

http://onewebday.org

OWD was born in the U.S., but it's starting to reach other countries.  See
if there's an event in your town.

http://onewebday.org/onewebday-events-map/

If not, please consider blogging or tweeting - #OWD09 - to help spread the
word.  Thanks for your time.

Best,
Matt
