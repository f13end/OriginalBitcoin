Dear friends, dear colleagues,

It is with  great pleasure that we introduce our  brand new and unique
/Postgraduate Studies in  ICT & Media Law/ programme,  which starts in
September 2010.

The  Postgraduate Studies  in ICT  &  Media Law  programme focuses  on
acquiring an  *advanced level  of knowledge* of  European ICT  & Media
Law, on gaining insights in a number of *disciplines* that are closely
connected to  ICT &  Media Law (technology,  economy, socio-political,
cultural and media studies), and on obtaining practical legal *skills*
in this field.

We  welcome   applications  from  *graduate  students*   who  want  to
specialise in  ICT & Media Law,  aspiring to a  professional career in
the public /  private sector or to an  academic (research) career, and
from *professionals* who want to update and expand their knowledge and
skills in the field of ICT & Media Law and related disciplines.

We   would    like   to   invite   you   to    consult   our   website
<http://www.law.kuleuven.be/icri/psiml.php>,    which   contains   all
information about the concept and  structure of the programme, and the
application  deadlines  and  procedures.   If  you  have  any  further
questions  regarding  the  programme,  please  contact  the  programme
director, Eva Lievens <mailto:eva.lievens at law.kuleuven.be>.

Best regards,

Prof. Dr. Jos Dumortier (Chairman and founder of ICRI),

Prof. Dr. Peggy Valcke (Director ICRI),

Dr. Eva Lievens (Director Postgraduate Studies in ICT & Media Law)
