I agree this is a great area.  I feel that most of the ownership models
needed are available in most countries.  Now they need to work to encourage
people toward co-ops, resource trusts (e.g. land trusts), NGO models, etc.

Freedom of expression, however, is always under threat.  Article 19 does
great work on this...and here is a link where people can endorse the Camden
Principles on Freedoms of Expression and Equality.

http://www.article19.org/advocacy/campaigns/camden-principles/index.html

I really admire and appreciate what Iceland is moving toward as a haven of
expression.  Great work!

Ryan
