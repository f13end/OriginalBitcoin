Since you already have it turned on and are evaluating, I'd suggest
checking out this URL too:
http://semantic-mediawiki.org/wiki/Help:Introduction_to_Semantic_MediaWiki#Where_SMW_can_help


Semantic media wiki extension allows you to create semiotic
relationships between objects.


Example: person(Michel Bauwens) relationship(lives in) place(Thailand)


This could change the way that you organize information, if enough
people will use it.
