Dale: Mark has written a remarkable summary of the necessary comm
infrastructures for p2p collaboration.

See
http://cyberlaw.stanford.edu/system/files/From+Wifi+to+Wikis+and+Open+Source.pdf

I have also been following your recent policy formulations.

Here's my wish for 2008: that we somehow could produce, a list of policy and
political aims, that are specifically geared to achieving a society based on
open and free, participatory, and commons-oriented values. This should not
necessarily be a complete program to change the whole of society, but rather
a specific one that could be added to already existing demands for equity in
society.

My second wish is to create a non US-centric list of policy advisors as well
as politicians who are open to defending these kind of positions ...
