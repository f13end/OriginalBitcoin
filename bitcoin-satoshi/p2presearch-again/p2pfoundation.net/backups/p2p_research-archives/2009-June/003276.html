 Dear Hub Community



The Hub has developed a partnership with Positive-Economy, in the framework
of their conference-debate on “How to build a positive economy”. The event
will demonstrate how* businesses can be run, while integrating social and
environmental values into their core processes*.



During the first part of the event, participants will hear testimonials from
European entrepreneurs, followed by a debate on the building of a positive
economy. After a networking lunch, workshop sessions will take place with
practical case studies, such as corporate values sustainable reporting,
industrial synergies, social economy model and global ecological process.


We would like to invite you to participate to this symposium (see invitation
below).

*The normal price is 240€ (+ VAT) for the day, however, Hub Members benefit
from a lower price of 120€ (+ VAT)!
*If you are not yet a Hub Brussels member and are interested to benefit from
that promotional price, please contact Alexander Riedl at
alexander.riedl at the-hub.be or Eric Theunis at eric.theunis at the-hub.be

*A positive economy is another approach of the economy, which respects
human, social and environmental values. An economy in harmony with society
and the world around us.*



The pioneers of positive economy have developed a profitable business that
brings a tangible contribution to the environmental and social challenges of
our society. With this symposium, Positive-Economy.eu, a project aimed at
promoting positive economy, introduces you to the new generation of
entrepreneurs.

Come and meet the future on 16th June 2009!
