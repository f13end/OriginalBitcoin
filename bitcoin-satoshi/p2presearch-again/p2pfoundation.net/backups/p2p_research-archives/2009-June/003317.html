*Introduction :

We are living in exceptional times. Scientists tell us that we have 10 years
to change the way we live, avert the depletion of natural resources and the
catastrophic evolution of the Earth's climate.

The stakes are high for us and our children. Everyone should take part in
the effort, and HOME has been conceived to take a message of mobilization
out to every human being.

For this purpose, HOME needs to be free. A patron, the PPR Group, made this
possible. EuropaCorp, the distributor, also pledged not to make any profit
because Home is a non-profit film.

HOME has been made for you : share it! And act for the planet.

Yann Arthus-Bertrand
