The idea is really simple...

If we think of what "power words" exist in our conscience, e.g. justice,
fairness, peer to peer, cooperation, etc then simply just give a contextual
definition of each (in the context of a personal or common p2p theory) then
it will be easy to explain in those plain and powerful words what P2P is ...

Patrick took it further with the mind map idea but at its root it's really
simple: any social paradigm can be explained in terms of its moral values,
and the moral values can be explained in terms of precise in-context
definitions of the 'power words' ...

I would personally go with the basic power words that are understood to
everyone, not any words that are specific to p2p, social theory or
economics.
