Dear all and especially Franz,
I hope you had a very happy easter or maybe just a great weekend!

I would like to invite you to a collective inquiry into the art of Hosting
Meaning in Place – exploring why more and more of us  - whether in urban or
rural locations -
are being called to:

• Host physical places such as learning & retreat centres, hubs,
eco villages, etc.
• Convene people in our organisations and communities to meet our complex
issues systemically
• Bring greater meaning into our close relationships, contexts and
family systems.

*When:* May 25-28
*Where:* Axladitsa Avatakia, a place for living wholeness in Greece, close
by the sea! :) http://www.axladitsa.org/place.htm
*Why: *To take some time for reflection and collective inquiry, slowing down
to speed up, connect to nature and to each other.

The calling question is "What do we know - as hosts of places and
initiatives - about the relationship between the powers of place and
cultivating our collective capacity to create a life- affirming world?"

Please find the invitation and registration attached. For more info, see
http://www.axladitsa.org/calendar.htm
RSVP to info at axladitsa.org, or directly to me if you prefer.
Economy should not be the reason for not coming! Please start a conversation
with us. We do have a few scholarships available.

Feel free to circulate this to people you know who might be interested in
joining us!

Warm regards from sunny Germany,
Simone

P.S. There are 2 other inquiries happening in Axladitsa in May: The Presence
retreat and The Art of Protection. Some more info in the attached flyer
"Axladitsa Immersion".
