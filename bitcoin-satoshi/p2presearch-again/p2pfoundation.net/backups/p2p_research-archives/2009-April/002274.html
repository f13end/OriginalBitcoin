I'm just going to comment on the aspect of Ryan's response that interests me
the most:

Visioning is NOT 'necessarily ideological' unless you want to sell the
vision, i.e. you have to believe that it's there (and just requires XYZ)
already in order for others to believe in it and fund it etc.

But the extent to which one believes their own imagination is what I'm
concerned about... sometimes it crosses the border between vision and
delusion, where the latter is defined as believing in your own vision beyond
just for show (beyond just to sell the vision)

Marc
