I would like to have a little constructive discussion on the topic of
*"enabling
artists to make a living without hampering creativity (and possibly while
stimulating it)"* (which already ditches the current copyright/royalties
system, so no need to discuss that particular point). I think it's nice to
keep a wide focus on the word "artist", even though current events give more
importance to the music field.

questions. Yet.

I'd like to do this through a *dedicated mailling list*, systematically
extracting the constructive elements *to a wiki page*.

Hopefully by the end of the discussion, we'll have a clearer idea of what
alternatives already exist, what is potentially doable, and how good each of
that is.
