> Scientists at the University of Liverpool have provided the first
> experimental evidence that shows that evolution is driven most powerfully by
> co-evolutionary interactions with other species, rather than adaptation to
> the environment. The team observed viruses as they evolved over hundreds of
> generations to infect bacteria. They found that when the bacteria could
> evolve defenses, the viruses evolved at a quicker rate and generated greater
> diversity, compared to situations where the bacteria were unable to adapt to
> the viral infection. (Source: http://www.physorg.com/news186311100.html)
