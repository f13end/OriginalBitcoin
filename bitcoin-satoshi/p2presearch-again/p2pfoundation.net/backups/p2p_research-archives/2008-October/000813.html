Hi Michel,

Also I started teaching at NYU Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP).
The course is called Creative Networking, an introduction to complex
networks within the context of the arts. In this class, we approach the
network itself as a creative medium. So we are trying to understand its
properties to be able to create new critical work. We put emphasis on
network topology, network modeling, creative protocol authoring, and
information design. Please see the full syllabus here:

http://blog.burak-arikan.com/courses/creative-networking/
