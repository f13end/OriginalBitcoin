We've been working on this at
http://socialsynergyweb.com/cgi-bin/wiki2/Multilingual/FrontPage since
around 2006

This interface lets you write in one language with a language "tag", then
place checkboxes in the language your want to read, and "switch". A language
switching wiki. It's based on OddMuse, and is a project primarily headed up
by Mattis Manzel, and programmed by me.
