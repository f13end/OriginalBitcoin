Dear Friends,

With your help, I would like to create a borderless piece of art.
Hobby, love, sport, politics, sex, friendship, passion, spirituality, small
secret or big hope,... on a postal card anonymously summarize what currently
gives a meaning to your life. Today, a topic, perhaps tomorrow a different
one. Go wild with your creativity but use just a few words.

Buy or, even better, create a postal card, stamp and send it to us:
Meaning of Life. Now
Casella Postale 8
86034 Guglionesi (CB)
Italie

The most original postal cards will be published on the project's website
www.meaningoflifenow.org (and later on perhaps they might be exhibited).

Please, feel free to circulate this info but please do not spam.
Thank you in advance for your support.

Kind regards,
