> [image: Open Everything logo]Please join us on October 30th for Open
> Everything: Ann Arbor. We are one of several locations hosting this
> worldwide event, during which participants take part in a global
> conversation <http://openeverything.wik.is/> about the art, science and
> spirit of ‘open’.  Open Everything gathers people using openness to create
> and improve software, education, government, media, philanthropy,
> architecture, food systems, workplaces and the society we live in. It’s
> about thinking, doing, and being open.
>
> Hosted by the Open.Michigan Initiative, Ann Arbor’s event will provide an
> evening of discussions around the ways that openness, collaboration, and
> participation are unfolding in our area and relate to the businesses,
> organizations, and networks we are a part of. Representatives from the Creative
> Commons <http://creativecommons.org/>, University of Michigan Copyright
> Office <http://copyright.umich.edu/>, Scholarly Publishing Office<http://www.lib.umich.edu/spo/>,
> a2Geeks<http://a2geeks.org/display/geek/Homehttp://a2geeks.org/display/geek/Home>and other local activists and entrepreneurs will be among the many
> individuals helping to launch this event. It’s an opportunity to connect
> with people who value openness—a chance to collaborate with the people
> involved in or creating projects and initiatives that are charting the
> trajectory of open here in Ann Arbor and beyond.
>
> If you value transparency, openness, access to information, strong
> communities, and sharing and have project ideas or other examples of where
> this ‘open’ ethos exists in our community, we look forward seeing you here!
>

> Date: Friday, October 30th, 2009
