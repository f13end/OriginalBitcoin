Hi Rasa,

I just created a separate section on distributed energy developments, which
could serve well for the energy reader and essay, and perhaps be useful for
the workshop as well,

see http://p2pfoundation.net/Category:Energy

some distinctions

* distributed energy as infrastructure, which includes smart grids and
social metering trends

* relation between energy and economics, with energy-related open currencies
and energy trading proposals

* policy proposals

* mobility and communication related (from ecars now to green wifi)

below the items we have documentation on, but also see the intro article on
the category page; we have a more extensive delicious tag on p2p=energy as
well

Michel
