Dear Advisors and Colleagues,

Our current whirlwind of activity attests to the green economy becoming
mainstream, along with ESG and triple bottom line investing!  I am one of

businesses, economists, development authorities, NGOs and scientists

years -- much less than the over $14 trillion of financial bailouts and less
than 10% of the $120 trillion of global pension funds' assets. (More

solar, wind, geothermal, renewables and energy efficiency so as to track the
accelerating growth of the green economy worldwide.
