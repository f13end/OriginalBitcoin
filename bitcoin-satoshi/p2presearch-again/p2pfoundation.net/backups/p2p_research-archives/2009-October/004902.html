Dear friends,

*State of the World Forum is an organizational partner of the Renaissance2
Great Shift Gathering <http://www.renaissance2.eu>* which will take place in
Perpignan, France from 22nd to 26th October, including a launch of the
WorldShift
Alliance <http://worldshift2012.org/content/alliance-for-a-new-humanity>.

*Renaissance2, *a not-for-profit foundation dedicated to catalyzing global
social innovation, *is inviting State of the World Forum members to join the
Great Shift Gathering* *by offering a 30% discount on tickets to the event.*

Use discount code *WorldShift* when registering to receive 30% off the fee.

The Gathering is designed around two main events.  *Event 1* focuses
on *Designing
a Resilient Civilization* and focuses on new models forming the basis of
Capitalism 2.0 and projects that can accelerate their adoption.  Event 2
emphasizes the importance of the role of *Conscious Evolution*, of shifting
ourselves, our organizations and our families and communities to the next
level of conscious co-creative capacity and the ability to act collectively
intelligent, in harmony with nature’s intelligence.

 Event page url is http://greatshiftgathering.eventbrite.com. You can use
the 30% discount code *WorldShift *by clicking on "Enter discount code".

Lodging Information can be found at this link: Travel information
pdf<http://www.renaissance2.eu/pdfs/R2%20Travel%20Information.pdf>

A limited number of bursaries are also available from the Renaissance2
Foundation to cover 50% of the event attendance fee for those who can
demonstrate their commitment and passion for work of Renaissance2. Contact
them at: enquiries at renaissance2.eu for further details.”

Best wishes,
