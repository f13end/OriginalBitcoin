*Swarmteams 2.0 released

I am pleased to let you know that we have just released Swarmteams  2.0.

Swarmteams 2.0 is a major extension which positions Swarmteams as the core
product which any enterprise (commercial, media, public or not-for-profit)
can use to manage their community engagement activities from a single point
via integrated email marketing, online community, social networking and
mobile phone. All data is integrated and totally under the community owner's
control with a full activity reporting subsystem built in.

Check out the new "This is Swarmteams" video (2 mins) :

Swarmteams 2.0 also introduces 'closed communities' which are private and
hidden from the general pool of open communities and can be branded and
customized. In addition 2.0 supports secure internet (https) and extends
interaction with mobile phones via a new swarmteams mobile browser
application as well as full SMS support.

For resellers and partners we can now provide branded and customized
versions of swarmteams using 2.0 customization and the Swarmteams API.
