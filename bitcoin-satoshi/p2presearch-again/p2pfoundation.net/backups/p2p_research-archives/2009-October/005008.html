Dear all,

as you might know the EC is running a Public Consultation on the policies to
be developed for post-i2010 in the context of the information society.

Here's their questionnair, you have until this Friday (9/10/9) to have your
voice heard. Let it be a strong collective voice!

http://ec.europa.eu/information_society/eeurope/i2010/pc_post-i2010/index_en.htm

You can answer parts or all of the questionnair. Here's an example from
the answers I put in (quickly, I'm sure you can do better!)

See here: http://freeknowledge.eu/wiki/index.php/EC-Post-i2010-Consultation

or below.

It costs some time, although most questions are optional so you can skip
whatever you don't feel like answering.
