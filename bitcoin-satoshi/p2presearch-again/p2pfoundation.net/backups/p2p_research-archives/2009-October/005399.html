The magicians Penn and Teller are apparently strongly conventional
libertarian/skeptics.  They have an HBO television show that
covers/represents their views.  They did one on organic foods.  It wasn't
pretty.  They did taste tests (with organic advocates) questioned food
experts, nutrition experts, etc.  The net of that story was that organics
were a social waste and not as good in any obvious way--pests, soils,
productivity, nutrition, health, tastes.  If I recall, Borlaug might have
been cited.  But since his death, I've seen him a lot...so I can't be
certain.

Just another data droplet tangentially associated.
