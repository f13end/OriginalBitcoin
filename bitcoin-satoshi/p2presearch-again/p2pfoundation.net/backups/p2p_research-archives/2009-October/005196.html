New World Library:

http://www.uusmaailm.ee/raamatukogu/

You can browse for books/movies/tools/other stuff* available for free
lending in the neighborhood, and communicate with the owner.

The system has grown quite rapidly - with the first 5 months we have 80
users, 70 movies and
150 books.

It is build as a Wordpress Plugin, so when we are ready with the development
we want to make it an opensource free download system, so everyone who wants
can download the system, and start a library in their neighborhood.

*other stuff - we are hoping someday somebody will offer a car for a small
